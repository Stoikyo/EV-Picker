## EV Picker Design Guidelines

- Fonts: headings use Fredoka (variable `--font-heading`), body uses Inter (variable `--font-body`).
- Spacing: base unit 8px (0.5rem). Stick to 8/16/24/32/40 for paddings/gaps where possible.
- Colors:
  - Primary: `#0A66FF`
  - Primary hover: `#084FC2`
  - Text (near-black): `#0F172A`
  - Muted text: `#475569`
  - Background/surface: `#F8FAFC` on sections, white cards
  - Border: `#E2E8F0`
- Buttons (4px radius):
  - Primary: filled blue background, white text, hover to primary hover
  - Secondary: white background, blue text + blue outline, hover to light blue tint
  - Text button: blue text only, no fill
- Components:
  - Cards: white background, 1px border in `#E2E8F0`, subtle shadow on hover.
  - Badges: pill shape, 12–14px text, light border, low-saturation backgrounds.
  - Sections: prefer max width ~1100–1200px, generous vertical padding (64–96px).
- Tone: calm confidence, buyer-first, avoid hype; concise copy and one clear action per section.
